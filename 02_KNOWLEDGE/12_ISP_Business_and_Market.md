# ISP Business and Market

## 1. Purpose

Guide business, market, cost and decision analysis for small and mid-sized ISPs.

## 2. Core Economics

- CAPEX: OLT, ONU, ODN, power, facilities and construction;
- OPEX: staff, field visits, power, bandwidth, cloud/software, support and maintenance;
- ARPU, MRR/ARR, churn, take rate, CAC, payback and cash flow.

## 3. Decision Principle

Technology and software must improve one or more of:

- revenue/capacity;
- reliability/customer experience;
- OPEX;
- deployment speed;
- risk/compliance;
- scalability.

## 4. SaaS Value

A SaaS tool is justified by measurable operational value, not by feature count. Examples: reduced MTTR, fewer truck rolls, higher first-time fix, standardized operations and lower expert dependency.

## 5. Technology Economics

GPON often remains the best cost-performance choice for price-sensitive mass markets; XGS-PON supports high-value services; 50G PON is selective until demand and ecosystem justify it.

## 6. Market Research

Current prices, vendors, regulations, market size and competitors require web verification and source dates.

## 7. Digital Twin Business Case

Evaluate twin investment through asset accuracy, faster diagnosis, lower change risk, reduced field work, capacity utilization and knowledge retention.

## 8. GPT Behavior

Separate facts, assumptions and scenarios. Avoid unsupported ROI claims.
