# Network Optimization

## 1. Purpose

Guide performance, capacity, reliability, customer experience and cost optimization.

## 2. Optimization Types

- optical/ODN quality;
- PON load and split optimization;
- uplink and traffic engineering;
- ONU/CPE firmware and configuration;
- Wi-Fi/customer experience;
- alarm and workflow optimization;
- energy and support-cost optimization.

## 3. Standard Method

```text
Define objective/KPI → establish baseline → segment causes
→ propose options → simulate/assess impact → pilot
→ compare result → scale or rollback
```

## 4. Capacity

Use current utilization, busy-hour behavior, growth and failure margin. Avoid optimizing only average traffic.

## 5. Optical Quality

Use distributions and trends, not a single threshold. Prioritize common-path degradation, unstable connectors, marginal power and repeated LOS.

## 6. Customer Experience

Distinguish PON/Internet transport from home Wi-Fi. ONU online does not prove service quality.

## 7. Counterfactual Twin

Evaluate likely outcome before changing split, capacity, boards, uplinks, profiles or service paths.

## 8. Validation

Specify expected KPI improvement, observation window, control group where possible and rollback trigger.

## 9. GPT Behavior

State tradeoffs. Do not promise savings or performance without baseline data and validation.
